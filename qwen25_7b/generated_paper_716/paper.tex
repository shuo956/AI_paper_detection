\section{Introduction}
The arXiv platform is a widely used repository for scientific papers, particularly in the fields of physics, mathematics, computer science, quantitative biology, quantitative finance, statistics, electrical engineering, and economics. Ensuring the integrity and security of user interactions is crucial for maintaining the credibility and accessibility of the platform. Traditional CAPTCHA methods, while effective in preventing automated spam and abuse, often lead to poor user experiences due to complexity and inconvenience. Modern reCAPTCHA v3 offers a more seamless user experience while maintaining robust security measures. This study aims to evaluate the performance of reCAPTCHA v3 on arXiv and propose enhancements to further improve the platform.

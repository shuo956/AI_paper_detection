\section{Methods/Experiments}
The proposed secure aggregation technique, named SecureFedAgg, incorporates differential privacy and homomorphic encryption to ensure data privacy and robustness. The method works as follows:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Each client encrypts its local model updates using homomorphic encryption.
    \item Encrypted updates are securely aggregated on the server without decryption.
    \item The aggregated update is then decrypted and applied to the global model.
\end{enumerate}

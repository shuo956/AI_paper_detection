Previous research has focused on various aspects of reCAPTCHA, including optimization of the reCAPTCHA algorithm \cite{ref1}, enhancing user experience through better design \cite{ref2}, and improving security measures \cite{ref3}. However, there is still room for improvement in terms of balancing usability and security.

\section{Methods/Experiment}
Our approach involves several key components:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Optimizing the reCAPTCHA Algorithm:} We propose a new algorithm that reduces false positives while maintaining high accuracy.
    \item \textbf{Improving User Interface:} We redesign the reCAPTCHA form to be more intuitive and visually appealing.
    \item \textbf{Integrating Multi-Factor Authentication:} We explore the integration of MFA to enhance security without sacrificing ease of use.
\end{enumerate}

To evaluate the effectiveness of our proposed system, we conducted a series of experiments involving real-world users. The results were compared against the current reCAPTCHA system.

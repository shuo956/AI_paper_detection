Recent advancements in deep learning have led to significant improvements in various natural language processing tasks, including neural machine translation (NMT). However, these models are often vulnerable to adversarial attacks, where small, carefully crafted perturbations to input sequences can cause the model to produce incorrect translations. This paper aims to address this issue by exploring the effectiveness of adversarial training techniques in enhancing the robustness of NMT models.

\section{Related Work}

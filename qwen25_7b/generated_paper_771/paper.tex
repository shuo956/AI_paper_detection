### Introduction
Object recognition in complex scenes remains a challenging problem in computer vision, particularly due to factors such as varying lighting conditions, occlusions, and partial visibility. Existing approaches, while effective in controlled settings, often fail to generalize well to diverse and challenging environments. To address these limitations, this paper introduces a new deep learning model designed to enhance robustness and accuracy in object recognition tasks.

### Methods/Experiment
Our proposed model, named Multi-Attention Multi-Scale Network (MAMSN), consists of three key components:
1. **Multi-Scale Feature Extraction**: The model employs multiple convolutional layers with varying receptive fields to extract features at different scales. This helps in capturing local and global contextual information.
2. **Attention Mechanism**: An attention module is integrated into the network to dynamically focus on salient regions within each scale. This mechanism helps the model to ignore irrelevant or less informative areas.
3. **Fusion Layer**: A fusion layer combines the outputs from different scales and attention modules to produce the final classification result.

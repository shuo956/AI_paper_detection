\section{Discussion}
The results indicate that the integration of customized reCAPTCHA mechanisms has generally improved user satisfaction and reduced completion times across all user groups. \section{Conclusion}
The study demonstrates that integrating customized reCAPTCHA mechanisms into university websites can enhance both security and accessibility.

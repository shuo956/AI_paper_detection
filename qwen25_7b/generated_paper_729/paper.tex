### Title
Enhancing User Engagement Through Adaptive ReCaptcha Systems in Online Academic Repositories

### Abstract
The increasing reliance on digital platforms for academic dissemination has necessitated robust security measures to prevent spam and abuse. This paper explores the implementation and evaluation of adaptive reCAPTCHA systems within online academic repositories. By analyzing user behavior and feedback, we aim to enhance the user experience while maintaining high levels of security. Our study compares traditional static reCAPTCHA methods with adaptive approaches that dynamically adjust the challenge level based on user interaction patterns. The results demonstrate significant improvements in user engagement and satisfaction without compromising security. This work contributes to the broader understanding of how adaptive reCAPTCHA systems can be effectively integrated into digital academic environments.

### Introduction
In the era of digital transformation, online academic repositories have become indispensable resources for researchers, educators, and students. However, these platforms face numerous challenges, including spam, abuse, and unauthorized access (Smith et al., 2021). One of the primary security measures employed to mitigate these issues is the use of reCAPTCHA systems. Traditional reCAPTCHA solutions often rely on static challenges, which can lead to user frustration and decreased engagement (Johnson & Lee, 2020).

Adaptive reCAPTCHA systems, on the other hand, leverage real-time user behavior analysis to adjust the challenge level dynamically. This approach aims to provide a more seamless user experience by reducing false positives and improving overall satisfaction (Brown et al., 2022). Despite their potential benefits, there is limited empirical evidence comparing adaptive reCAPTCHA systems with their static counterparts in the context of academic repositories.

This paper seeks to fill this gap by conducting a comprehensive study to evaluate the effectiveness of adaptive reCAPTCHA systems in enhancing user engagement and satisfaction. We propose an adaptive reCAPTCHA framework that integrates machine learning algorithms to analyze user interactions and dynamically adjust the challenge level. The study involves a controlled experiment to compare the performance of adaptive and static reCAPTCHA systems, with a focus on metrics such as user engagement, satisfaction, and security.

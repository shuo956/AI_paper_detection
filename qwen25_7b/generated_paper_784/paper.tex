Deep learning models have achieved remarkable success in various fields, particularly in image classification tasks. However, their lack of interpretability and susceptibility to adversarial attacks pose significant challenges. This paper addresses these issues by proposing a method to integrate explainable AI (XAI) techniques into pre-trained models, thereby enhancing both their performance and robustness.

\section{Related Work}
Existing approaches to improving interpretability and robustness in deep learning models include saliency maps, LIME, and SHAP. Additionally, various techniques such as adversarial training and defensive distillation have been used to increase model robustness. However, these methods often come at the cost of reduced performance or increased complexity.

\section{Introduction}

Large language models have achieved impressive performance on various natural language processing tasks. However, their reasoning capabilities are limited by their reliance on language priors rather than fine-grained visual dynamics. To address this limitation, we propose a novel approach that combines counterfactual reasoning and visual conditioning to enhance the reasoning capabilities of LLMs.
